League Bowling

There are tenpin bowling leagues to suit everyone.
We have leagues for children, adults, seniors and also people with a disability.
You can form your own team or join a team that is looking for a bowler.
League bowling takes place on Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday nights,
Thursday mornings and Saturday afternoons.
Our leagues compete for trophies & prize money all while having fun!

Give us a call today or simply email us at info@planettenpin.com.au
