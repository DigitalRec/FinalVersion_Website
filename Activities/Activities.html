Planet Tenpin

Tenpin bowling fun for everyone!
At Planet Tenpin bowling is our passion. We know tenpin bowling
is a sport for everyone. We are about participation and encourage
all abilities from beginners to pros. We have a full service
tenpin bowling pro shop providing coaching and equipment – ask
for Bruno anytime. We work to provide a friendly and welcoming
atmosphere whether it’s your first time on our lanes or you
are a league bowler.
